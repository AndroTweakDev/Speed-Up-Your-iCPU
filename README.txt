**** Speed Up Your iCPU "SUYiCPU" 1.0 ****

--- How to install: --- 

iOS 3.x - 4.x: 

Step 1: With an File explorer (like iFile / iFunBox) go to System/Library/SystemConfiguration/PowerManagement.bundle/
Step 2: Rename com.apple.SystemPowerProfileDefaults.plist in com.apple.SystemPowerProfileDefaults.plist*
Step 3: Put modded com.apple.SystemPowerProfileDefaults in System/Library/SystemConfiguration/PowerManagement.bundle/
Step 4: Reboot your iDevice
Step 5: Respring your iDevice

iOS 5.x :

Step 1: With an File explorer (like iFile / iFunBox) go to System/Library/CoreServices/Powered/SystemPowerProfiledDefaults
Step 2: Rename com.apple.SystemPowerProfileDefaults.plist in com.apple.SystemPowerProfileDefaults.plist*
Step 3: Put modded com.apple.SystemPowerProfileDefaults in System/Library/CoreServices/Powered/SystemPowerProfiledDefaults
Step 4: Reboot your iDevices
Step 5: Respring your iDevice
